Well, technically, it's a reboot of my old mod...
### Thalassa
You are in command of a core that is stranded on Thalassa, a distant planet, covered mostly in oceans.
However, it seems that there's an enemy faction that has already begun harvesting its resources and developing rapidly.
The arms race has begun, and you must play catch-up.
### Embark
Your crash landing site is in a hot spring, near a small Dread research outpost.
Use the steam released by geysers to power basic machinery and establish a base.
### ⚠ Compatibility Warning ⚠
Replaces Blue Team with Dread. Other mods might do so too, causing overlap. Consequences unknown.
